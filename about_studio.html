About Studio

MoonGaze Studio is a game development studio focused on creating immersive and engaging games on the Roblox platform. Our mission is to bring unique gaming experiences to players worldwide, inspired by popular culture and innovative gameplay mechanics.

Founded in 2025, MoonGaze Studio has quickly grown to become a recognized name in the Roblox community. We are passionate about game development and strive to push the boundaries of what is possible within the Roblox ecosystem.

Developers

Our team consists of talented developers, designers, and artists who work collaboratively to bring our game concepts to life. We believe in fostering a creative and inclusive environment where ideas can flourish.

We are always looking for new talent to join our team. If you are passionate about game development and want to be part of an exciting studio, please reach out to us through our contact page.

Testers

We value the feedback of our community and have a dedicated team of testers who help us refine our games before release. Testers play a crucial role in identifying bugs, suggesting improvements, and ensuring that our games provide the best possible experience for players.

If you are interested in becoming a tester for MoonGaze Studio, please visit our contact page for more information on how to apply.
